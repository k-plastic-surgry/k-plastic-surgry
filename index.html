Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is a groundbreaking solution for patients experiencing early hair loss or thinning. This minimally invasive treatment uses the patient’s own plasma to stimulate hair follicles, encouraging natural growth and improving hair density. PRP therapy is ideal for individuals seeking a safe and effective alternative to traditional hair restoration techniques.

The process involves drawing a small amount of the patient’s blood, isolating the plasma rich in growth factors, and injecting it into targeted areas of the scalp. Over time, PRP therapy revitalizes dormant follicles, resulting in thicker, healthier hair.
